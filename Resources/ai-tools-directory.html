AI Tips for Students & Freelancers

Start with free tiers

Most AI tools have generous free plans. Master them before upgrading to paid versions.

Learn prompt engineering

Better prompts = better results. Be specific, give context, and iterate on your prompts.

Always verify AI output

AI can hallucinate. Double-check facts, code, and references before submitting work.

Combine tools strategically

Use ChatGPT for drafts → Grammarly for polish → Canva for visuals. Build your own AI workflow.
