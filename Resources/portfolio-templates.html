Portfolio Tips from Our Mentors

  • Quality over quantity — showcase 3-5 best projects
  • Write project case studies, not just screenshots
  • Include measurable results where possible
  • Keep your portfolio updated every month
  • Optimize for mobile — clients review on phones
  • Add a clear call-to-action (hire me / contact)
