About KCA Ajira Club

We empower KCA University students with practical digital skills, mentorship and pathways to freelancing, remote work and entrepreneurship.

Our Mission

To equip students with in-demand digital skills and connect them to global opportunities, enabling financial independence and career readiness.

Our Vision

To be Kenya's leading student digital empowerment community, driving inclusive participation in the digital economy.

What We Do

  • Weekly hands-on Skill Labs (web, design, marketing, data, AI tools)
  • Freelance Launchpad to build profiles & win clients
  • Mentorship and alumni support for real projects
  • Portfolio & interview preparation
  • Industry partnerships & job linkages
