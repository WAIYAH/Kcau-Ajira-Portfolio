About KCA Ajira Club

We are a student-led initiative at KCA University dedicated to preparing students for the digital economy through practical skills training, mentorship, and real-world project experience.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To equip KCA University students with in-demand digital skills, connecting them to global opportunities in freelancing, remote work, and digital entrepreneurship.

Vision

To become Kenya's premier student digital empowerment community, producing highly competitive digital professionals who contribute to Kenya's digital economy growth.

Alignment with Kenya Vision 2030

We directly contribute to Kenya's vision of a knowledge-based economy by developing digital talent and promoting innovation among youth.

Why Join Ajira Club?

  • Practical, hands-on digital skills training
  • Access to freelancing and remote job opportunities
  • Mentorship from industry professionals
  • Portfolio development with real projects
  • Networking with like-minded ambitious students

Our Programs & Offerings

We offer structured pathways to help you transition from learning to earning in the digital economy.

Weekly Skill Labs

Hands-on sessions covering digital marketing, web development, content creation, data analysis, and AI tools.

  • Every Wednesday, 5-7 PM
  • Practical project-based learning
  • Industry expert guest sessions

Freelance Launchpad

Step-by-step guidance to start freelancing on platforms like Upwork, Fiverr, and LinkedIn.

  • Profile optimization workshop
  • Proposal writing & client management
  • Portfolio building support

Digital Mentorship

1-on-1 mentorship pairing with industry professionals and successful alumni.

  • Career path guidance
  • Skill development roadmap
  • Networking opportunities
