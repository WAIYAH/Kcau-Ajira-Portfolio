1 Our Commitment

KCA Ajira Club is dedicated to providing a safe, welcoming, and harassment-free experience for everyone—regardless of gender, gender identity, age, sexual orientation, disability, physical appearance, body size, race, ethnicity, religion (or lack thereof), or technology choices.

Our Vision: We believe in the power of diverse perspectives, empathetic collaboration, and mutual respect. Every member enriches our community—and every member deserves to feel valued and supported.

2 Expected Behavior

We expect all members, mentors, and participants to:

Be Inclusive

Welcome newcomers, use inclusive language, and respect diverse perspectives.

Communicate Respectfully

Give constructive feedback kindly. Disagree without being disagreeable.

Support Each Other

Share knowledge, help beginners, and celebrate others' achievements.

Act with Integrity

Be honest about your work, respect intellectual property, and give credit.

Be Mindful

Consider the impact of your words and actions on others in the community.

Respect Privacy

Don't share others' personal information without their consent.

3 Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors will not be tolerated in any KCA Ajira Club space:

Harassment & Intimidation

Including offensive comments related to personal characteristics, deliberate intimidation, stalking, following, or unwanted photography/recording.

Discrimination & Hate Speech

Any form of discrimination or exclusion based on gender, ethnicity, religion, disability, or any other protected characteristic.

Sexual Harassment

Including unwelcome sexual attention, inappropriate comments, and sharing sexually explicit content.

Plagiarism & Dishonesty

Claiming others' work as your own, submitting AI-generated work without disclosure, or misrepresenting your capabilities.

Disruption & Trolling

Sustained disruption of events, workshops, or online discussions; trolling or deliberately provoking others.

5 Reporting & Enforcement

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, please report it. All reports are kept confidential.

How to Report

1

Email us: conduct@kcauajira.club

2

Speak to a leader: Approach any club officer or mentor in person

3

Anonymous form: Use our contact form (select "Report a Concern")

No Retaliation: We strictly prohibit retaliation against anyone who files a report in good faith. Retaliatory behavior will be treated as a separate violation.

6 Consequences

Violations of this Code of Conduct may result in actions ranging from a warning to permanent ban, depending on severity:

Level 1 — Warning

Private written notice explaining which behavior was inappropriate and why.

Level 2 — Temporary Suspension

Temporary removal from club activities and online spaces (duration depends on severity).

Level 3 — Permanent Ban

Permanent removal from all KCA Ajira Club spaces, events, and communications.

7 Support Resources

If you need support—whether from a conduct issue or personal difficulty—these resources are available:

KCA Student Affairs

Counseling, academic support, and welfare services for all KCA University students.

Mental Health Support

Confidential peer support and professional referrals available through the wellness office.

Club Leadership

Club officers are trained in conflict resolution and available to help mediate disputes.

Emergency Contacts

For immediate safety concerns: KCA Security (+254-xxx-xxx-xxx) or Kenya Police (999).

8 Updates to This Code

We may update this Code of Conduct to reflect new community needs and best practices. Changes will be announced through our communication channels and posted on this page.

Questions about this Code? Contact us at conduct@kcauajira.club
