1 Introduction

KCA Ajira Club ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you interact with our website, programs, events, and services.

This policy applies to all members, visitors, and users of our website and services. By accessing our website or registering for our programs, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

2 Data We Collect

We collect information to provide and improve our services. Here's what we collect:

Personal Information

  • Full name, email address, and phone number (when you register or contact us)
  • University/organization name and student ID (for membership verification)
  • Profile photo (optional, for member profiles)
  • Program enrollment and attendance records

Usage Data

  • Pages visited, time spent, and navigation patterns
  • Browser type, device information, and IP address
  • Referring website and exit pages

Cookies

  • Session cookies for authentication and navigation
  • Preference cookies for remembering your settings
  • Analytics cookies for understanding usage patterns (see Section 4)

3 How We Use Your Data

We use your information for the following purposes:

Membership Management

To register you as a member, manage your account, and track program enrollment.

Communication

To send you program updates, event invitations, newsletters, and important announcements.

Analytics & Improvement

To understand how our website and programs are used so we can improve the experience.

Safety & Security

To protect our community, prevent fraud, and ensure compliance with our Code of Conduct.

4 Cookies & Tracking

Our website uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us remember your preferences and understand how you use our site.

Type Purpose Duration
Essential Required for site functionality and login Session
Preferences Remember your settings and choices 1 year
Analytics Understand usage patterns and improve our site 2 years

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some website functionality.

5 Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously. We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Our Security Measures Include:

  • Encrypted data transmission using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure servers with restricted access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Staff training on data protection best practices
  • Incident response procedures for data breaches

6 Your Rights

Under the Kenya Data Protection Act (2019) and GDPR (where applicable), you have the following rights:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.

Right to Rectification

Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.

Right to Erasure

Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").

Right to Opt-Out

Unsubscribe from marketing communications at any time via the link in our emails.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@kcauajira.club. We will respond within 30 days.

7 Third Parties

We may share limited data with trusted third parties who help us operate our services:

  • KCA University — For student verification and academic partnership coordination
  • Ajira Digital Program (Government of Kenya) — For program reporting and impact measurement
  • Google Analytics — For anonymous website usage statistics
  • Email service providers — For sending newsletters and updates

We never sell your personal data to third parties. All partners are contractually bound to protect your data.

8 Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our website. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when the policy was last revised. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
