Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our programs.

Do I need prior experience?

No. We offer beginner-friendly pathways and hands-on guidance for every level.

How long are the programs?

Most programs run between 4-12 weeks depending on the track and cohort.

Are programs free?

Yes, core programs are free for members. Premium tracks may have small fees.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes. Completion certificates are provided for structured cohorts.
