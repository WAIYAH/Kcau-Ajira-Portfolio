Frequently Asked Questions

How do I join the club?
Click the "Join Now" button on any page and fill in your details. Registration costs KES 200, plus KES 100 per semester. You can also visit us during any meeting session on Fridays or Thursdays from 2:00 – 4:00 PM.
How long does it take to get a response?
We typically respond to emails within 24–48 hours during weekdays. For urgent matters, attend a meeting in person or reach out to any of our leadership team members directly.
Do I need any experience to join?
No prior experience is needed. Our programs are designed for all skill levels — from complete beginners to advanced learners. We'll help you find the right track based on your interests and goals.
