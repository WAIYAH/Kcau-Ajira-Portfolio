Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best way to reach you?
For general inquiries, email is best (hello@kcauajira.club). For urgent issues, call us during business hours or email conduct@kcauajira.club. Check the contact channels section above to find the right email for your specific inquiry.
How long does it take to get a response?
Response times vary by inquiry type. General inquiries: 24-48 hours. Partnerships: 2-3 business days. Technical support: 4-6 hours (during business hours). Urgent matters: same day. See the "Expected Response Times" section for more details.
Can I call you directly?
Yes! Call us at +254 (0) 701 234 567 during business hours (Mon-Fri 9 AM-5 PM, Sat 10 AM-2 PM EAT). For non-urgent matters, email is preferred as it helps us respond more efficiently.
How do I submit a partnership proposal?
Email partnerships@kcauajira.club with your proposal details. Include information about your organization, the partnership opportunity, and what value you can bring to our community. We review all proposals and respond within 2-3 business days.
What should I do if I experience harassment or discrimination?
We take safety seriously. Report any violations to conduct@kcauajira.club or call us immediately. All reports are confidential, investigated promptly, and handled with care. You can also reach out to any leadership team member. Zero tolerance for harassment.
Can I visit your office?
Yes! We're located at KCAU campus in Nairobi. Visit us during business hours (Mon-Fri 9 AM-5 PM, Sat 10 AM-2 PM EAT). For group visits or meetings, email hello@kcauajira.club 48 hours in advance so we can arrange proper accommodations.
Do you respond to comments on social media?
We monitor our social media accounts and respond when we can, but email is more reliable for important inquiries. For urgent matters, use direct email addresses instead. Social media is best for general engagement and updates.
